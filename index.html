About

Laksh Bhatia is a researcher, innovator and engineer specializing in wireless systems with a focus on Integrated Sensing and Communication (ISAC) in 6G and beyond cellular networks. I am presently working as an ETSI ISG ISC WI#4 Delegate. My experience spans distributed systems for 6G, IoT systems, indoor localization, asset tracking, and cyber-physical systems. I am an experienced embedded systems engineer with expertise in developing end-to-end IoT solutions.
